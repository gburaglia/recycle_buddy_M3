Introduction:

Interactive AI Application with Teachable Machine, P5.js, and Ml5.js called Recycle Buddy. Created for the class LMC 8803 GM1 AI 4 DM taught by Brian Magerko and Manoj Deshpande at Georgia Tech. I wanted to provide a prototype AI model that would help consumers sort through their individual trash and facilitate proper recycling. While simultaneously teaching them through visual indicators when an item is categorized.

For the first milestone, the model could classify an object as belonging to 1 of 5 categories, after being trained on ~1000 images per category. For the second milestone, in the case of identifying a platic object, the user could further specify type of plastic by holding up their fingers.

How to Interact:

This interactive project makes use of your webcam to help you sort your recyclables

  • Starting Up: Ensure your webcam is active as the project requires it to identify your recyclable object.
  • Classifying: Hold up your object to know what it is made of and if it can be recycled.
